Safety for all of our customers

SafeTravels has the mission to care for everyone who rents a hotel with us. That is why we make a very close check to ensure that our hotel partners are taking all the hygienic and covid-related measures we request of them.

We want our customers to feel safe and cared for. We want you to be happy and to value your investment on us as a rewarding choice.

We have come up with a list of basic regulations for all the hotels that you can book in our site. You can find extra basic regulations in each of their websites or contact them to know more about it.

This is a list of those measures that our hotel partners must fulfill:

  • Hidroalcoholic dispensers available
  • Frequent cleaning in common areas
  • Exhaustive ventilation of rooms, restaurants, and other common areas
  • Controlled capacity in all zones
  • Mandatory use of mask in common areas
  • Furniture cleaning increase in pool areas
  • Pool water disinfection increase
  • A compromise with service digitalization (food and service menus accesible online)
